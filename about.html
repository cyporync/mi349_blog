About the author

Hi, I'm Claire Cyporyn. I am a senior at Michigan State University majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Computational Mathematics, Science, and Engineering. My cognate area of focus is Media and Information. I'm passionate about my field because I love problem solving and coming up with creative solutions. I enjoy designing and building both the frontend and the backend of applications. I currently work for the State of Michigan in the Department of Technology, Management, and Budget as a student cybersecurity analyst.

In my free time, I enjoy playing guitar, practicing yoga, listening to music, seeing my friends, reading, and writing. I like to collect CDs. I also like to cook, and I enjoy trying new recipes.
